# Section Language Rules

Apply only the rules for sections in scope. These are language controls, not scientific templates.

## Title And Abstract

- Improve precision, economy, and readability without adding a construct, context, method, result, or contribution absent from the manuscript.
- Preserve numerical values, sample scope, effect direction, and uncertainty.
- Do not strengthen novelty, causality, or generalization to make the abstract more decisive.

## Introduction

- Reduce generic importance language, repetition, vague antecedents, and mechanical transitions.
- Preserve the approved phenomenon, research question, gap type, context, and contribution scope.
- Do not repair a missing gap, create urgency, or restructure the central storyline. Flag those issues for the responsible Skill.

## Literature Review And Theory

- Improve conceptual clarity and local progression while preserving definitions, theoretical premises, mechanisms, construct boundaries, and citation attachment.
- Do not substitute near-synonyms for distinct constructs or compress away rival explanations and boundary conditions.

## Hypotheses

- Preserve hypothesis IDs, variable roles, direction, mediator or moderator form, population, condition, and outcome.
- Do not turn reasoning into stronger causal or universal language.
- Do not rewrite a scientifically ambiguous hypothesis without author authority.

## Methods

- Improve procedural clarity, tense consistency, referents, and reporting precision.
- Preserve design, sample, exclusions, conditions, measures, manipulations, sequence, software, models, and analysis designations.
- Do not correct, complete, or redesign a method. Flag apparent gaps or contradictions.

## Results

- Improve reporting clarity and consistency while preserving every value, sign, estimate, interval, p value, sample, model, comparison, table or figure reference, and support designation.
- Do not calculate, round, interpret, reconcile conflicts, infer mediation or moderation, or convert exploratory findings into confirmatory findings.
- Keep Results distinct from theoretical interpretation unless the journal structure supplied by the user explicitly integrates them.

## Discussion And Contributions

- Improve clarity, cohesion, calibration, and repetition while preserving the relation between findings, interpretations, contributions, limitations, and recommendations.
- Do not introduce an absent mechanism, hide a null result, strengthen causality, expand generalization, or upgrade empirical/contextual novelty into theoretical contribution.

## Limitations And Future Research

- Preserve the seriousness, scope, and consequences of each limitation.
- Do not minimize a limitation for tone or create a future-research direction absent from the source.

## Tables, Figures, Notes, And Supplements

- Preserve numbering, labels, values, symbols, significance notes, units, construct names, and cross-references.
- Improve language only. Do not change data, order, or scientific classification.

## Simplified Chinese

- Preserve academic register and intentional technical English.
- Do not convert the task into conversational humanization or AI-authorship detection.
- Do not apply rules from `$qu-ai-wei` unless the user separately requests that capability in a separate run.
